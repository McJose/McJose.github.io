MANAGEMENT

Head of ICT

Our head of ICT ensures that the IT infrastructure and operations of the organization are aligned with Mwalimu sacco's strategic goals and objectives. With network security officers on the team, a duty to ensure that the organization's network operations are aligned with the organization's specifications is also bestowed in him.


ICT Manager

The ICT Manager is responsible for coordinating and planning activities in the department.She spear-heads large ICT projects and the design and deployment of new IT systems and services. The Manager ensures that the IT department collaborates with other departmnents to align technology and the company's business goals. Another crucial responsibility of the manager is to safeguard data security and privacy.


Senior ICT Officer

Overseeing the implementation of the Society's ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system is one key role the senior ICT officer does. Other roles are; Planning and implementing information systems infrastructure, Ensuring network security aligns with established ICT policies and finally administering the mobile communications infrastructure of the SACCO


Network and Security Officer

The ICT network and security officer conducts internal and external penetration tests on the SACCO'S systems. The SACCO networks are on a 24-hour active monitoring and analysis under the watch of the Network and security officer. Whenever a network threat is encountered he is responsible for ensuring that all staff members are informed.

Assistant Network and Security Officer

As an Assistant Network and security officer he supports the management of information security, network infrastructure, and related hardware. Other roles are: Assist in conducting internal and external penetration tests on the SACCO's platforms and raise awareness among staff and stakeholders about cybersecurity matters.
